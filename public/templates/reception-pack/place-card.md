# Reception Pack: Place Card

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 3.5″ × 2″ (89mm × 51mm) — standard tent fold
- **Bleed:** 0.125″ (3mm) on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 0.2″ (5mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Landscape (horizontal) — fold into tent

## Style Description
Compact tent-fold place card with guest name in large serif text and table number in a sage circle. Gold accent line across the bottom. Designed to stand on its own at each place setting.

## Production Specs
- **File format:** PDF (print-ready, merge-ready)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Paper:** 110lb (300gsm) matte cardstock
- **Size:** 3.5″ × 2″ (tent fold; stands at 1.75″ tall)
- **Setup:** Fold in half crosswise to create tent
