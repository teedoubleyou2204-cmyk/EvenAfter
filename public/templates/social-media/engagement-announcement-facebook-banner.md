# Social Media Template: Engagement Announcement — Facebook Banner / Cover

## Dimensions
- **Size:** 1640px × 624px (20:7.6 — Facebook cover photo ratio)
- **Mobile Safe Zone:** 1080px × 462px (centered, visible on mobile)
- **Profile Photo Safe Zone:** Bottom left, 170px × 170px circle area
- **Format:** Static image
- **Platform:** Facebook page banner, Facebook personal profile cover

## Style Description
Horizontal banner layout ideal for announcing an engagement on Facebook. Features a photo on the right side with elegant text on a sage-toned left panel. The profile photo area is designed to frame the couple's faces without overlapping critical text.

## Color Application
- **Left panel background:** Sage (#9CAF88)
- **Left panel text:** White (#FFFFFF) and Warm Gold (#C9A94E)
- **Right panel:** Photo with subtle dark overlay for contrast
- **Footer:** Charcoal (#3A3A3A) text on Ivory (#FDF8F0)

## Customization Notes
- The profile photo circle area (bottom left) must remain text-free — Facebook overlays the avatar here
- For personal profiles, move the text higher to avoid the profile photo overlap
- The photo on the right can be swapped for a venue shot or a flat-lay of stationery
- Ensure the photo is high resolution (min 1640px wide) to avoid pixelation on desktop

## Production Specs
- **File format:** PNG (optimized for web)
- **Resolution:** 1640×624px @ 72 DPI
- **Color mode:** RGB
- **Max file size:** < 1MB for Facebook upload
- **Critical safe zone:** Keep all text within 100px of left edge, above the bottom 200px
