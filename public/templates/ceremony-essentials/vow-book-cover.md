# Ceremony Essentials: Vow Book Cover

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 4″ × 6″ (101mm × 152mm) — mini booklet cover
- **Alternative:** 5″ × 7″ for larger vow books
- **Bleed:** 0.125″ (3mm) on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 0.35″ (9mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Portrait (vertical) — wrap-around cover

## Style Description
Elegant cover for a DIY vow book. Features the couple's names and wedding date with a sage border and gold accents. The inside can hold blank pages for handwritten vows, or print the included vow template pages.

## Customization Notes
- Print 4-6 inside pages with the vow template lines, then bind with ribbon or staple
- Add the couple's initials as a monogram instead of the floral icon
- For a thinner book, use 2-3 inside pages (vows only, no additional prompts)
- The cover can be printed on heavier cardstock than the inside pages

## Production Specs
- **File format:** PDF (print-ready, cover + inside pages)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Paper:** 80lb (170gsm) matte cover for cover; 32lb (120gsm) for inside pages
- **Binding:** Saddle-stitch, ribbon tie, or spiral bound
- **Size:** 4″ × 6″ (fits in a clutch or pocket)
