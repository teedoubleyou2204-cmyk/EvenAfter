# Stationery Expansion: Thank You Card

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 5″ × 7″ (127mm × 178mm) — or 4.25″ × 5.5″ (A2)
- **Bleed:** 0.125″ (3mm) on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 0.25″ (6mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Portrait (vertical) — folded card

## Style Description
Elegant thank-you card with a sage border and gold accents. Features space for a photo on the front (or left panel) and a heartfelt message inside. Perfect for post-wedding thank-you notes.

## Customization Notes
- For bulk thank-you notes: keep the cover identical, customize the inside message per guest
- Instead of a photo, use a decorative monogram or the couple's initials
- The card can be flat (unfolded) with the message on the back

## Production Specs
- **File format:** PDF (print-ready, folded card layout)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Paper:** 80lb (170gsm) matte cover stock
- **Size:** 5″ × 7″ (folded to 5″ × 3.5″) or A2 (4.25″ × 5.5″)
- **Envelope:** A7 or A2 matching invitation suite
