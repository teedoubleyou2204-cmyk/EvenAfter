# Invitation Template: Modern Asymmetric

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 5″ × 7″ (127mm × 178mm) — standard invitation card
- **Bleed:** 0.125″ (3mm) on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 0.25″ (6mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Portrait (vertical) — asymmetric split

## Style Description
Contemporary split-layout design with a bold color block on one side and clean typography on the other. Perfect for modern couples who want a sleek, editorial look. Uses negative space as a design element.

## Design Elements

| Element          | Description                                        |
|------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| Left Panel       | Full-height sage (#9CAF88) color block — 40% width |
| Right Panel      | White (#FFFFFF) background — 60% width             |
| Gold Icon        | Single gold diamond or circle motif on left panel  |
| Divider          | Thin gold rule (horizontal, right panel mid-section)|
| Vertical Divider | 0.5pt gold line separating the two panels          |

## Color Application
- **Left block background:** Sage (#9CAF88)
- **Left block text:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Right panel background:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Names & venue:** Charcoal (#3A3A3A)
- **Date emphasis:** Sage (#9CAF88)
- **Dividers & icon:** Warm Gold (#C9A94E)

## Customization Notes
- Swap left panel color to match any wedding palette (blush, navy, terracotta)
- The gold icon on the left panel works beautifully as a minimal line-art flower, a heart, or an ampersand
- For a bolder look, increase left panel to 50% width
- Add a subtle sage watercolor wash behind the right panel text for extra depth

## Production Specs
- **File format:** PDF (print-ready) / PNG (digital)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Color mode:** CMYK for print, RGB for digital
- **Paper:** 110lb (300gsm) premium matte cardstock
- **Envelope:** A7 (5.25″ × 7.25″) — white or sage
