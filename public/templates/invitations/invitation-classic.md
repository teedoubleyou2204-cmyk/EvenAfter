# Invitation Template: Classic Elegance

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 5″ × 7″ (127mm × 178mm) — standard invitation card
- **Bleed:** 0.125″ (3mm) on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 0.25″ (6mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Portrait (vertical)

## Style Description
Traditional symmetrical layout with a central focus. Features an ornate gold-toned border frame, elegant script heading, and clean serif body text. Ideal for formal evening weddings.

## Design Elements

| Element      | Description                                          |
|--------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Border       | 0.125″ gold line border, 0.25″ from card edge       |
| Header Motif | Circular ornate frame with monogram or floral center |
| Dividers     | Thin gold horizontal rule with diamond accent center |
| Footer Box   | Rounded rectangle with thin gold stroke (0.5pt)     |
| Background   | Ivory (#FDF8F0) solid fill                          |

## Typography

| Text Level              | Font              | Size    | Color     | Alignment |
|-------------------------|-------------------|---------|-----------|-----------|

| Venue                   | Montserrat Light  | 9pt     | Charcoal  | Center    |
| Reception / Details     | Montserrat        | 8pt     | Warm Gold | Center    |
| RSVP                    | Montserrat        | 9pt     | Charcoal  | Center    |

## Color Application
- **Main text:** Charcoal (#3A3A3A)
- **Date text:** Sage (#9CAF88)
- **Accent lines/dividers:** Warm Gold (#C9A94E)
- **Card base:** Ivory (#FDF8F0)
- **RSVP box stroke:** Sage (#9CAF88)

## Customization Notes
- Replace `[NAME OF COUPLE]` with the couple's full names (e.g., "Emily Rose & James Alexander")
- Update date, time, venue, and RSVP info
- Option to swap gold accents for rose gold or silver by changing the Warm Gold hex to #E8B4B8 or #A8A8A8
- The floral motif at top can be replaced with a custom monogram or left as a decorative frame

## Production Specs
- **File format:** PDF (print-ready) / PNG (digital)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Color mode:** CMYK for print, RGB for digital
- **Paper:** 110lb (300gsm) matte cardstock
- **Envelope:** A7 (5.25″ × 7.25″) — matching ivory or sage
