## 1. Top Platforms for Selling Wedding Printables

### Primary Marketplace: Etsy
- **Position:** Dominant marketplace for wedding printables with 90M+ active buyers
- **Fees:** $0.20 listing fee per item + 6.5% transaction fee + 3% + $0.25 payment processing
- **Traffic:** Wedding category generates ~15M organic views/month
- **Optimal for:** Invitation templates, seating charts, checklists — the exact products in our lineup
- **SEO advantage:** Etsy ranks #1 on Google for "wedding invitation templates" and related searches
- **Strategy:** List products individually AND as bundled collections (e.g., "Ultimate Wedding Stationery Bundle")

### Secondary Marketplace: Creative Market
- **Position:** Premium design marketplace for professional-quality templates
- **Fees:** Curated application process; 40% commission on first $100K, 25% thereafter — OR flat $15/month shop subscription
- **Traffic:** Smaller but higher-intent audience (professional designers, serious planners)
- **Optimal for:** Premium invitation suites, the brand style guide, Canva templates
- **Pricing range:** Products typically sell $12–$29 (higher than Etsy)
- **Strategy:** Apply for shop membership once we have 5+ polished products; price at premium tier

### Niche Platforms (Tier 2)

| Platform | Best For | Fee Structure | Notes |
|----------|----------|---------------|-------|
| **Gumroad** | Direct-to-customer sales | 8.5% + $0.30 (free tier) or $10/month + 3.5% | No marketplace discovery — need own traffic. Good for own website backend |
| **The Knot Marketplace** | Wedding-specific exposure | Vendor fees vary | High credibility; potential to partner as "preferred vendor" |
| **Zazzle / Minted** | Print-on-demand | Commission-based (10-15%) | Less control over pricing and branding |
| **Templett** | Template marketplace | Commission ~30-40% | Niche for Canva/Photoshop templates |
| **Design Cuts** | Design bundles | Commission ~50% per bundle sale | Good for once-off bundle promotions |

### Channel Strategy Recommendation
1. **Primary:** Etsy — list all products (individual + bundles)
2. **Secondary:** Gumroad + Own Website (to drive repeat traffic and higher margins)
3. **Tertiary:** Creative Market — premium tier once brand is established
4. **Occasional:** Design Cuts or similar bundle sites for promo events

## 3. Marketing Strategy to Reach DIY Couples

### Channel #1: Pinterest (Highest ROI — 85% of users plan events)

#### Keywords to Target
- "DIY wedding invitations" — 45K monthly searches
- "Printable wedding invitations" — 33K monthly searches
- "Wedding checklist printable" — 22K monthly searches
- "Wedding seating chart template" — 18K monthly searches
- "Canva wedding invitation template" — 15K monthly searches
- "Boho wedding invitations" — 12K monthly searches
- "Modern wedding stationery" — 10K monthly searches
- "Wedding budget spreadsheet" — 8K monthly searches

#### Content Strategy
1. **Pin each template** as a vertical image (1000×1500px) with overlay text showing the benefit
2. **Create "Idea Pins"** (video carousels) showing customization steps — "How to edit this in Canva in 3 minutes"
3. **Publish "Complete Wedding Planning" boards** — group our templates with complementary planning content
4. **Seasonal pins:** "Spring Wedding Collection," "Winter Wedding Printables"
5. **SEO-rich pin descriptions:** include phrases like "editable Canva template," "DIY," "instant download"

#### Posting Cadence
- 5–10 new pins/day (mix of static + Idea Pins)
- Use Tailwind (scheduler) to maintain consistency
- Join group boards with 10K+ followers in the wedding niche

### Channel #2: Instagram

#### Hashtag Strategy (Mix of sizes)
- **Broad:** #weddingplanning (30M+), #weddinginspo (25M+), #diywedding (5M+)
- **Niche:** #weddingprintables (450K), #weddingstationery (2.1M), #canvatemplate (1.2M)
- **Location-targeted:** #citynamebride (e.g., #chicagobride) — high engagement
- **Community:** #etsywedding (900K), #weddingtiktok (cross-post)

#### Content Calendar (Weekly)

| Day | Content Type | Theme | Example |
|-----|-------------|-------|---------|
| Monday | Carousel | "How to" | "5 steps to design your wedding invitations in Canva" |
| Wednesday | Reel | Transformation | Before (messy notes) → After (beautiful template) |
| Friday | Static post | Product showcase | Highlight a specific template with story |
| Saturday | Story poll | Engagement | "Which color scheme? A. Sage B. Blush C. Gold" |
| Sunday | User repost | Social proof | Share customer's finished invitation (with permission) |

#### Growth Tactics
- **Collaborations:** Tag wedding planners, venues, and photographers who might share our content
- **Freebie lead magnet:** "Free wedding packing checklist" downloadable via link in bio
- **Micro-influencer outreach:** DM 15–20 micro-accounts (5K–20K followers) offering free templates for honest reviews

### Channel #3: TikTok (Emerging Opportunity)

- **Content:** "Day in the life of wedding planning," "Watch me design my invitations in 30 seconds"
- **Trend jacking:** Use trending sounds + wedding planning fails/transitions
- **Best format:** Fast-paced transformation reels (ugly-to-beautiful template editing)
- **CTA:** Link in bio to Etsy/Gumroad shop

### Channel #4: Google SEO & Blog Content

| Content Type | Target Keyword | Traffic Potential |
|-------------|----------------|-------------------|

| "Wedding Planning Checklist: 12 Month Timeline" | wedding planning checklist | Very High |

### Channel #5: Email Marketing

- **Sequence:**
  1. Welcome email + free download link
  2. Day 3: "3 templates every engaged couple needs"
  3. Day 7: Bundle offer with 20% discount code
