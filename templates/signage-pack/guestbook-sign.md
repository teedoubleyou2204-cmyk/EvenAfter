# Signage Pack: Guestbook Sign

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 5″ × 7″ (127mm × 178mm) — small tent or frame
- **Bleed:** 0.125″ (3mm) on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 0.25″ (6mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Portrait (vertical)

## Style Description
A small, elegant sign placed next to the guestbook, inviting guests to leave a message. Features the couple's names, a gold border, and a warm, personal message.
