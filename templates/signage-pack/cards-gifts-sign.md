# Signage Pack: Cards & Gifts Sign

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 5″ × 7″ (127mm × 178mm)
- **Bleed:** 0.125″ (3mm) on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 0.25″ (6mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Portrait (vertical)

## Style Description
A charming sign directing guests where to place cards and gifts. Features a sage header bar and delicate gold accents.
