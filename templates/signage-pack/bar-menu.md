# Signage Pack: Bar Menu / Drink Sign

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 8″ × 10″ (203mm × 254mm)
- **Alternative:** 11″ × 14″ for larger bars
- **Bleed:** 0.125″ (3mm) on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 0.5″ (12mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Portrait (vertical)

## Style Description
Elegant bar menu with a sage-to-blush gradient header, featuring signature cocktails, wine, beer, and non-alcoholic options. Gold divider lines separate drink categories. Perfect for the bar or cocktail hour table.

## Design Elements

| Element            | Description                                        |
|--------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| Gradient Header    | Sage (#9CAF88) to Blush (#E8C4C4) top bar          |

| Background         | #FFFFFF           | Ivory, Cream, or photo       |

## Customization Notes
- Adjust drink categories to match your bar (add a "Whiskey" or "Vodka" section for open bars)
- For open bars with no pricing, remove the price column
- Add a QR code linking to a custom cocktail recipe page

## Production Specs
- **File format:** PDF (print-ready)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Paper:** 80lb (170gsm) matte cover stock
- **Display:** Small easel, clip frame, or tent card on bar
