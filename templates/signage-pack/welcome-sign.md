# Signage Pack: Welcome Sign

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 16″ × 20″ (406mm × 508mm) — large sign
- **Alternative:** 11″ × 14″ or 20″ × 24″ (scale proportionally)
- **Safe Zone:** 0.75″ (19mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Landscape (horizontal)

## Style Description
Large welcome sign with a sage green header bar, ivory background, and elegant centered text. Features "Welcome to Our Wedding" in large serif font, the couple's names, and a brief thank-you message. Designed for framing or mounting on an easel.

## Customization Notes
- Customize the welcome message (add a Bible verse, poem excerpt, or fun greeting)
- For outdoor weddings, print on water-resistant paper or foam core mount
- The sign can be scaled to poster size (24×36) for larger venues

## Production Specs
- **File format:** PDF (print-ready, tiled for home or single file for print shop)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Paper:** 100lb (270gsm) matte cover stock or foam core mount
- **Display:** Easel stand, acrylic frame, or clip frame
