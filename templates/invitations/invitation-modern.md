# Invitation Template: Modern Asymmetric

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 5″ × 7″ (127mm × 178mm) — standard invitation card
- **Bleed:** 0.125″ (3mm) on all sides
- **Safe Zone:** 0.25″ (6mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Portrait (vertical) — asymmetric split

## Style Description
Contemporary split-layout design with a bold color block on one side and clean typography on the other. Perfect for modern couples who want a sleek, editorial look. Uses negative space as a design element.

## 🎨 Color Customization (How to Change Everything)

**This design's hero element is the colored left panel — swap it first for maximum impact.** No colors are locked.

### Quick Color Swap (3 Steps)
1. **Swap the main panel color:** Click the sage left panel → Color swatch → Enter your wedding primary color (e.g., `#2C3E50` Navy, `#C87D6E` Terracotta, `#E8C4C4` Blush)
2. **Match the date & vertical divider** to your panel color (or keep gold for contrast)
3. **Toggle text color:** If you choose a dark panel color, keep text white. If a light panel, swap text to Charcoal (#3A3A3A) for readability

## Customization Notes

- For a bolder look, increase left panel to 50% width
- Add a subtle watercolor wash behind the right panel text for extra depth
