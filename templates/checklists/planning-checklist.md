# Checklist Template: 12-Month Wedding Planning Timeline

## Dimensions
- **Print Size:** 8.5″ × 11″ (US Letter / A4)
- **Bleed:** 0.125″ (3mm)
- **Safe Zone:** 0.5″ (12mm) from edge
- **Layout:** Portrait (vertical) — 2 pages

## Style Description
Comprehensive month-by-month planning checklist organized by category. Each month has a cross-functional list covering venue, vendors, attire, decor, and logistics. Checkbox circles in sage for a satisfying check-off experience.

## Content Summary (Full Checklist Covers)
- **12 months out:** Budget, guest list, venue, planner, date
- **10 months out:** Photographer, caterer, attire, hotel blocks, florist, music
- **8 months out:** Save-the-dates, officiant, bridal party attire, engagement shoot, honeymoon
- **6 months out:** Invitations, registry, cake tasting, hair/makeup trial, transportation
- **4 months out:** Send invites, finalize menu, order rings, ceremony details, rehearsal dinner
- **2 months out:** RSVP deadline, final fittings, seating chart, vendor confirmations
- **1 month out:** Marriage license, final payments, timeline review, welcome bags
- **2 weeks out:** Confirm all vendors, pack for venue, rehearsal run-through
- **Week of:** Final count, vendor tips prepared, ceremony rehearsal, relax!

## Customization Notes
- Delete or add rows as needed — each month has 5–8 items
- Add your own custom line items for venue-specific tasks
- Change checkbox to squares or hearts by swapping the graphic element
- Print double-sided to keep all 12 months on one sheet or two pages

## 🎨 Color Customization

This checklist uses Sage and Warm Gold as accent colors — both fully swappable.

### Color Swap Mapping

| Design Element          | Default     | Swap To (Example)           |
|-------------------------|-------------|------------------------------|
| Header box fill          | #9CAF88 Sage | Navy, Blush, Terracotta     |
| Header text              | #FFFFFF White | Keep white (on dark fill)  |
| Month header rule       | #9CAF88 Sage | Match to primary color      |
| Month header dot accent | #C9A94E Gold | Your accent color           |
| Checkbox circle stroke  | #9CAF88 Sage | Primary or accent color     |
| Tip footer text          | #C9A94E Gold | Accent color                |

### Quick Step (Canva)
1. **Header box:** Click the sage header → Color → Your wedding color
2. **Checkboxes:** Click one checkbox circle → Select all same color → Change in one click
3. **Month headers:** Click the month rule line → Color → Your accent
