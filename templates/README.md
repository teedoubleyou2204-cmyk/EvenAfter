## Client Customization Instructions

### How to Use These Templates

1. **Open in Canva** — All designs use standard Canva-compatible dimensions.
2. **Replace Text** — Double-click any text field and replace the placeholder text with your own details.
3. **Swap Colors** — Use Canva's color picker to match your wedding palette. Our Sage/Blush/Gold scheme works with most color palettes.
4. **Change Fonts** — We recommend keeping Playfair Display for headings and Montserrat for body text, but Canva offers the full Google Fonts library.
5. **Add Photos** — Each template has photo placeholder areas where you can drop in engagement photos or venue shots.
6. **Export for Print** — Export as PDF (Print) at 300 DPI. For home printing, select "PDF (Standard)" at 300 DPI.
7. **Paper Recommendations**:
   - Invitations: 110lb (300gsm) matte or linen cardstock
   - Schedules & Checklists: 32lb (120gsm) premium laser paper
   - Seating Chart Display: 100lb (270gsm) cover stock or foam core mount

### Editing Software
- **Recommended:** Canva (free), Adobe InDesign, or Affinity Publisher
- **Alternative:** Microsoft Word or Google Docs (basic editing)
- **Print-ready:** Export all files at 300 DPI minimum, CMYK color mode for professional printing
