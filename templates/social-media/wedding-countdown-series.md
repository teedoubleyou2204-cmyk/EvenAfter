# Social Media Template: Wedding Countdown Series — Instagram Posts (3-Part Series)

## Dimensions
- **Size:** 1080px × 1080px (1:1 square per post)
- **Safe Zone:** 100px from all edges
- **Format:** 3-post carousel or 3 individual posts over time
- **Platform:** Instagram feed (carousel or sequential posts)

## Style Description
A cohesive three-part countdown series building excitement toward the big day. Each post uses the same layout template with different color accents and messaging — Sage (3 months out), Blush (1 month out), Gold (1 week out). Designed to be posted in sequence for a beautiful grid effect.

## Customization Notes
- Post all three at once as a carousel for a single high-impact moment
- Or post sequentially to build engagement over 3 months
- Each post can also be adapted for Stories with swipe-up CTAs
- Swap emojis (💚 → 💗 → 🎉) to match the tone progression

## Production Specs
- **File format:** PNG (3 separate files or 1 carousel-ready set)
- **Resolution:** 1080×1080px @ 72 DPI (each)
- **Color mode:** RGB
- **Captions:** Suggest 100-150 characters, 3-5 relevant hashtags
